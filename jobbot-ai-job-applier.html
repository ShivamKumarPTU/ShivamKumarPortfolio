JobBot — AI Job Applier

A semi-automated job application engine that scrapes 20+ portals, scores every job against your profile using Claude AI (0–100), auto-fills applications, generates cover letters, and drafts follow-up emails. Bot does 90% of the work. You click Submit.

What JobBot Does

🔍

Scrapes 20+ Job Portals

Every 8 hours, fetches jobs from Greenhouse, Lever, RemoteOK, LinkedIn, Naukri, iimjobs, HackerNews, and more.

🧠

Claude AI Scoring

Every job is scored 0–100 against your profile using Claude 3.5 Sonnet. You only see the best matches.

🤖

Auto-fills Applications

For Greenhouse & Lever ATS, Playwright auto-types your details into forms. You review, then click Submit.

📝

Cover Letter Generator

Generates a personalized cover letter for each job using your profile + the job description via Claude AI.

📧

Follow-up Email Drafts

7 days after applying, JobBot drafts a follow-up email so you never miss a chance to re-engage recruiters.

🔔

Discord Alerts

Pings your Discord when a high-match job lands (score 80+) so you act fast on the best opportunities.

Two-Tier Apply System

Tier Sources What Happens
✅ Auto-Apply Greenhouse, Lever Bot fills form visibly → pauses → you review → you click Submit
⚠️ Manual Only Naukri, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, iimjobs JobBot scores & tracks it → you click "Open Application →"
"Bot handles 90% of the work. You spend 2 minutes reviewing, then click Submit. Human stays in the loop. No account bans."
